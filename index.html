Brisbane - 23/08/2012

The boys and girls from Inexcess and Divinylz team up with the delightful Jo Elbers creating a brand new tribute band. Remember Countdown? Well come and see Countdown Reloaded, our brand new show. A high energy tribute to both Australian and International Artists of the unforgettable and for many, sadly missed Countdown Era. Countdown Reloaded play a selection of local Aussie hits of the day, along with plenty of the International artists. Stay Tuned for more info or visit our facebook page.
